DELLA ADVENTURE PARK

Della Adventure Park is an Indian amusement park located in the state of Maharashtra. It was started in 2013 by the Della Group and is spread across 36 acres in Kunegaon, Lonavala. In addition to over fifty recreational activities including adventure sports and glamping activities, the park also houses Della Resorts, a resort with hotels, villas, and five restaurants. According to Conde Nast Traveller India, it is India's largest recreation park.

Boasting of a plethora of activities including Aqua Zorb, swoop swing, flying fox, rappelling, paintball, buggy ride, bull ride, land zorbing etc, the park also has a nightlife club and several restaurants both for fine dine and binge eating.

Della Adventure Park in Lonavala is one of the best weekend getaways near Mumbai and Pune, it has everything to keep you excited with its various adventure activities for individuals as well as for groups.
